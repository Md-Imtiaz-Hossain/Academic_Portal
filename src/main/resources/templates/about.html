About XYZ Academic Portal

Introduction: In today’s day and age, we are more and more relying on technology in almost every scope of our life. This trend is increasing rapidly. We are shopping, ordering foods, renting cars etc. online, and using digital payment methods to pay for them. But one area of our society which has seen little to no digitalization is our academic life. Most schools still keep and store records in papers and manage every academic activity manually / in person. School fees and other economic transactions are handled manually which may lead to incorrect data handling which in turn wastes the student or the handling party’s time. Digitalization in this area will have huge impact on our upcoming generation.

Our project “School-College Management System” is an example in which way any school/college or equivalent academies can digitalize their whole academic management system efficiently. Everyone will be able to visit the academy’s site and get all the basic information and notices. After logging in, teachers will be able to manage their classes, students’ information, scheduling exams, results etc. Students will be able to see their everyday routines, update their profile, check their results, view their academic expanses etc. Other staffs will be able to manage the notices, economic transactions, admissions, alumni management, student applications. There will also be automated online payment system with manual options. And above all, the Head and Assistant Head Teacher will overlook the whole system and effectively fill the role of Site Admin.

Scope: The scope of this project will be schools or colleges inside Bangladesh. Most schools and colleges are still using pen and paper records to manage all academic activities and transactions. Our project will replace most of the manual work and automate most of the processes which will be accessible anywhere online. Our project’s main focus is on Bangladeshi schools and colleges. It may not be suitable for university management or higher academic systems. After slight modifications, this project might work in other countries.
